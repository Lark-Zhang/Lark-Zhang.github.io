训练的目的:找到最小化C(w,b)C(w,b)C(w,b)的权重和偏置.
假设C=C(v1,v2)C=C(v_1,v_2)C=C(v1​,v2​)

ΔC≈∂C∂v1Δv1+∂C∂v2Δv2\Delta C \approx \frac{\partial C }{\partial v_{1}} \Delta v_{1} + \frac{\partial C}{\partial v_2} \Delta v_2 ΔC≈∂v1​∂C​Δv1​+∂v2​∂C​Δv2​

定义CCC的梯度为偏导数的向量,(∂C∂v1,∂C∂v2)T(\frac{\partial C}{\partial v_1},\frac{\partial C}{\partial v_2})^T(∂v1​∂C​,∂v2​∂C​)T,定义Δv≡(Δv1,Δv2)\Delta v \equiv (\Delta v_1,\Delta v_2)Δv≡(Δv1​,Δv2​)

∇C≡(∂C∂v1,∂C∂v2)T\nabla C \equiv \left( \frac{\partial C}{\partial v_1}, \frac{\partial C}{\partial v_2} \right)^T ∇C≡(∂v1​∂C​,∂v2​∂C​)T
